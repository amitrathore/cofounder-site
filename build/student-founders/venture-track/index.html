Venture Track

A deeper build for students ready to be treated like founders.

The Venture Track is application-only and designed for students who can commit about ten hours a week to a more ambitious venture.

Compare Tracks Apply

Best fit

A student who already shows unusual drive, has a serious idea or domain interest, and is ready for 1:1 mentor pressure around execution quality.

Mentorship model

Students get a higher-touch build environment with individual guidance on positioning, product decisions, outreach, and venture narrative.

  • Approximately ten hours per week
  • 1:1 mentor attention
  • Application review before acceptance

Expected output

The goal is not a school project. It is a venture with a clear audience, a testable offer, and a credible next step after the cohort ends.

  • Sharper customer and market definition
  • Prototype or service path with evidence
  • Case study and founder narrative
