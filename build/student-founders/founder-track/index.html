Founder Track

A serious first venture, built around a realistic student schedule.

The Founder Track is designed for students who can commit about five hours a week and need a guided path from idea to evidence.

Compare Tracks Enroll

Best fit

A student with curiosity, initiative, and a parent who wants a structured commitment without overloading the school year.

Weekly rhythm

Students work through one venture milestone at a time, then bring decisions and evidence back for mentor review.

  • Approximately five hours per week
  • Group mentorship and structured reviews
  • Clear weekly deliverables

What changes

The student learns how to make progress without waiting for perfect certainty. They practice narrowing a problem, testing an audience, and shipping a useful first version.

  • Problem selection and customer discovery
  • AI-assisted prototyping habits
  • Founder communication and case-study writing
