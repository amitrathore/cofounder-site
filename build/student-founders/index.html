Student Founders

A 12-week path from curious student to real founder.

A structured program for students ready to turn initiative into a company-shaped project, with mentorship, modern AI tooling, and a clear weekly cadence.

Compare Tracks See Student Examples

Built for parents evaluating seriousness.

This is not a generic entrepreneurship club. Students work toward a concrete venture, learn how to use AI responsibly, and leave with evidence of judgment, persistence, and independent execution.

What students build

Each student chooses a problem, validates an audience, assembles a working prototype or service, and learns how to explain the venture clearly.

  • A venture thesis and audience map
  • A working prototype, service offer, or launch-ready experiment
  • A public-facing case study suitable for applications and interviews

How support works

The program creates adult-grade structure without taking over the student’s work. Mentors guide decisions, review progress, and keep the standard high.

  • Weekly milestones and review rituals
  • Responsible AI workflows and founder judgment
  • Community context with other builders
